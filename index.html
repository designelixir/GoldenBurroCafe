If you miss the Glory Days of Leadville's Golden Burro Café & Lounge...

From 1938 until 2019, the Golden Burro Café and Lounge stood as one of Leadville's favorite eating establishments.

A full length, 60 minute, DVD (produced in 2021), captures the entire story of this nationally famous restaurant from it's inception in 1938 until its sale in 2019, including a rare interview with Roy Pray, founder of the Golden Burro Café & Lounge, shortly before he passed away.

Many rare photos and video are included in this production. Click the below link to learn how to obtain a copy of this production, as well as other Leadville historical videos.
